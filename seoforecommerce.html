Understanding SEO

SEO means Search Engine Optimization, and it is the process of assessing the current on and off page factors of a website. Improving these factors will help boost rankings in a search engine such as Google, Bing, Yahoo, and so on.
Optimization is an act, process, or methodology of making something such as a design, system, or decision as fully perfect, functional, or effective as possible. You can optimize the keywords, meta description, headings, alt tags, inbound links, and page titles to further boost your ranking on search engines. Your ranking is the result of many factors, combined to determine a total grade.
You must focus on getting traffic in order to build rankings. You can drive more traffic through social media by linking through your sites.

Understanding On-Page vs. Off-Page SEO

On-page versus off-page SEO refer to the changes that you can make directly on the site itself versus things that are done on external locations that drive traffic to your site.
